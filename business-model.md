# Business Model Document
## ApnaDarzi.pk - Online Custom Tailoring Platform

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**ApnaDarzi.pk** is an online platform that connects customers in Pakistan with skilled tailors for custom-stitched clothing. The business model focuses on making traditional tailoring services accessible through digital means, offering convenience, quality, and personalized service.

### Vision
To become Pakistan's leading online custom tailoring service, making perfectly fitted, custom-stitched clothes accessible to everyone.

### Mission
To combine traditional tailoring expertise with modern technology, providing customers with easy access to quality custom clothing at affordable prices.

---

## 2. Business Model Overview

### Business Type
**B2C (Business-to-Consumer) E-commerce Platform**

### Core Value Proposition
- **For Customers:**
  - Convenience: Order custom clothes from home
  - Perfect Fit: Custom measurements for every order
  - Quality: Expert tailors with years of experience
  - Affordability: Competitive pricing
  - Variety: Wide range of designs and customization options

- **For Business:**
  - Scalable online platform
  - Reduced overhead costs
  - Direct customer relationship
  - Data-driven insights
  - Market expansion potential

## 3. Target Market

### Primary Market
- **Geographic:** Pakistan (nationwide)
- **Demographics:**
  - Men and women aged 18-55
  - Middle to upper-middle class
  - Urban and semi-urban areas
  - Internet-savvy individuals

### Market Segments

1. **Men's Segment:**
   - Shalwar Kameez (traditional wear)
   - Formal wear for office/events
   - Casual everyday wear

2. **Women's Segment:**
   - Formal dresses (parties, weddings)
   - Casual wear
   - Traditional Pakistani dresses
   - Designer wear

### Market Size
- Pakistan's population: ~240 million
- Growing middle class with increasing purchasing power
- Rising internet penetration
- Increasing preference for online shopping

## 4. Revenue Streams

### Primary Revenue Sources

1. **Product Sales (Main Revenue)**
   - Custom-stitched clothing orders
   - Price range: PKR 5,000 - PKR 30,000+ per order
   - Average order value: PKR 12,000-15,000

2. **Premium Services (Future)**
   - Express delivery (additional fee)
   - Premium fabric options
   - Designer consultation services
   - Home measurement service

3. **Fabric Sales (Future)**
   - Direct fabric sales
   - Fabric + stitching packages

### Pricing Strategy
- **Competitive Pricing:** Match or slightly below market rates
- **Transparent Pricing:** Clear pricing for all services
- **Value-Based:** Price based on fabric quality and complexity
- **Volume Discounts:** Discounts for bulk orders (future)

## 5. Cost Structure

### Fixed Costs
- Website hosting and maintenance
- Office/warehouse rent (if applicable)
- Salaries (admin staff, customer service)
- Marketing and advertising
- Technology infrastructure

### Variable Costs
- Fabric and materials (per order)
- Tailor wages (per order)
- Packaging and shipping
- Payment processing fees
- Customer service per order

### Cost Breakdown (Estimated per order)
- Materials (fabric, thread, etc.): 40-50%
- Labor (tailoring): 25-30%
- Overhead (admin, marketing): 10-15%
- Shipping: 5-10%
- Profit margin: 15-20%

## 13. Financial Projections (Estimated)

### Year 1 Projections

**Revenue Assumptions:**
- Average orders per month: 50-100
- Average order value: PKR 12,000
- Monthly revenue: PKR 600,000 - 1,200,000
- Annual revenue: PKR 7,200,000 - 14,400,000

**Cost Assumptions:**
- Fixed costs: PKR 200,000/month
- Variable costs: 70% of revenue
- Net profit margin: 15-20%

**Break-even Analysis:**
- Break-even point: ~40-50 orders/month
- Expected to reach profitability in 3-6 months

### Growth Projections
- **Year 2:** 150-200 orders/month
- **Year 3:** 300-400 orders/month
- Expansion to more cities
- Additional product lines
